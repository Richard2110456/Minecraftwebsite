The Most Popular Game of all time


Minecraft is an extremely popuar game thus it has an extensive multiplayer scene. Minecraft has these player made servers that you can join just by adding the IP. You can do PVP or play all different types of minigames.


One of these such servers is hypixel. One hypixel you can play most modes popular on minecraft multiplayer. You can do everything from survival games to skyblock. Survival games is a game mode where the player collects items from the chests and uses them to kill other players. SKyblock is like an MMO where you can progress through different armor builds and weapons to eventually get to the most powerful. The games are constantly updated.


Another one of these servers is The Arcon. It has most of the models hypixel has and another one caleed factions. In factions, you and other players are divided by teams and build massive bases to encase a game objective. YTou get armor and weapons to defend this objective and the team who captures the other team's goal first wins.
